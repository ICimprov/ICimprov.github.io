Who we are

Our founder, Baroque Capital's CEO Han Chiang, began trading in 2018 and founded Baroque Capital in 2022, and has been a member of a number of exchanges. Han is also the head of HFT trading team and option trading team in Baroque Capital.
Baroque Capital is a proprietary trading firm with a strong focus on data-driven solutions and automation. We design and implement sophisticated quantitative models in trading research through mathematics, statistics, and deep learning. Our group is a proprietary quant trading team from Chicago. We design and implement our own low latency technologies, trading strategies and mathematical models. Baroque Capital has no clients or external investors, we trade only with the company’s capital.
Baroque Capital is always looking for talented individuals. We seek energetic, motivated, and creative individuals to join our team. As a part of our team, you will have the opportunity to work with some of the best minds in the industry, as well as to be a key player in the company's success.

What we do

Baroque Capital specilizes in market making, high frequency trading, statistical arbitrage and capture various arbitrage oppotunities across asset classes in financial markets, including futures, options, cryptos, commodities, and fixed income markets. No matter the method by which trades are executed, our proprietary trading models and pricing technology is continually updated to keep us highly competitive in an ever changing global marketplace. Furthermore, Baroque Capital is not just a liquidity provider, a risk taker or a latency-sensitive trading firm. We are all of those things. In some markets, we’re all of them at once. In other markets, we focus on just one or two of these trading approaches. This is possible because we have some of the sharpest engineers, technologists, traders, quants and professionals in the world.
